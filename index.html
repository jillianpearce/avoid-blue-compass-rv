A factual consumer experience record

Our RV was totaled by water damage that occurred due to repairs the dealership made before we purchased it.

We purchased a 2022 Venture Sonic SN211VDB from Blue Compass RV on December 18, 2023. Before we took delivery, repairs had already been performed on the unit. We were told those repairs were minor, which was false. The RV had suffered major water damage on the Blue Compass lot due to a manufacturing defect and an improperly sealed roof. Less than two years later, the RV was deemed a total loss after new water intrusion was found in the exact area of those repairs.

What happened

This site documents our purchase, the repairs made before delivery, our maintenance and inspections, the discovery of water intrusion, and our attempts to resolve the issue with the dealer and manufacturer.

Who is involved

Blue Compass RV was the dealer. KZ RV is the manufacturer. Zurich is referenced in relation to insurance information. This site focuses on documented facts and our personal experience.

Why this site exists

We are at a standstill with an RV that was totaled by water damage before we had owned it for two full years. We are publishing the timeline and supporting documents so others can understand what happened.
