Timeline

This timeline documents the events that led to our 2022 Venture Sonic being declared a total loss less than two years after purchase.

Before December 18, 2023

Major defects repaired before delivery

Before we took delivery, the RV had already undergone major repairs due to manufacturing defects. An improperly sealed roof caused significant water intrusion and damage, and the slide room had been incorrectly measured and installed at the factory. We were advised that the repairs were minor, not informaed of any water damage, and that the issues had been resolved before delivery.

December 18, 2023

Purchase date

We took delivery of the new 2022 Venture Sonic SN211VDB from Blue Compass RV, believing the pre-delivery repairs had been properly completed and that the RV was in new condition.

During ownership

Maintenance and inspections

We performed routine maintenance and regularly inspected the RV and roof. Because we monitored the condition of the RV, we recognized that the later water intrusion was recent and not the result of long-term neglect or deferred maintenance. The RV was seen by Blue Compass in Loveland multiple times for other things before that branch closed permanently.

Discovery of water intrusion

Water damage found

Water intrusion was later discovered in the same general area associated with the pre-delivery repairs in December of 2025. The damage was extensive and raised serious concerns about the effectiveness of the original repair work.

Insurance determination

RV deemed a total loss

The dealership's insurance company, Zurich, performed an investigation of the course of several months in early 2026. They refused to pay on the claim for the new damages. Due to the extent of the water damage, the RV was ultimately determined to be a total loss, and a repair estimate of over $22,000 was given to us in May of 2026.

Current status

Unresolved dispute

Despite repeated efforts to resolve the matter with both Blue Compass RV and KZ RV, responsibility for the failure remains disputed, leaving us with a totaled RV less than two years after purchase. Our comprehensive RV coverage will not cover the damages either because they state that the responsibility lies with the manufacturer, KZ, and the dealership, Blue Compass RV.
